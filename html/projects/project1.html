STM32

STM32, C++ | 2024

My team and I designed and created a SumoBot for a school project and competition. It was mainly made out of syrofoam boards and aluminum tape, and used tamiya motors, infrared sensors and proximity sensors.

The SumoBot was coded in C using Arduino. We placed second overall in the competition, defeating multiple other teams.

Created by Aaron Sun, 2025.
